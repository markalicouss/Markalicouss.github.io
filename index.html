About Me

My name is Mark, I am a veteran and natural mechanic who loves off-roading. I've been all over the western US in my 4Runner. It is my passion to help others build their offroad dream rigs. My personal view on vehicles tend to be a bit conservative, as in i mostly do fireroad or powerline road riding. So I personally believe a 1.75'' lift and some good offroad tires will get you to 99% of places you want to go.

I am an expert in welding, suspension alignment, geography, and gearing ratios. I also offer maintenance services as well as troubleshooting. If I can’t do it, then it can’t be done!
