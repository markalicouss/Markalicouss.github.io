About TrailBlaze Customs

My name is Mark, and I am a veteran and natural mechanic who loves off-roading. I've traveled across the western US in my 4Runner and take pride in helping others build their offroad dream rigs. My approach is practical—most offroad adventures can be conquered with a 1.75" lift and solid all-terrain tires.

With expertise in welding, suspension alignment, geography, and gearing ratios, I also offer maintenance and troubleshooting services. If I can’t do it, then it can’t be done!
